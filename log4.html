Hermitian Matrix Diagonalisation and Solving Simple Quantum Systems

For this entry, I accomplished two main things: I studied and coded methods to diagonalise matrices; I applied these methods to simple problems in quantum mechanics. The meat of this shall be discussed in the iPython notebooks that are linked. I have decided to start using these notebooks for a clearer correspondence between the theory and the code.

Matrix Diagonalisation- Notebook , HTML
Application to Quantum Mechanics- Notebook , HTML

In doing this, I have learned:
- The Householder-QR Method of diagonalising Hermitian matrices
- The method of diagonalising tridiagonal matrices by properties Sturm sequences
- The convergence properties of the Householder method
- How to apply the Householder method to generalised eigenvalue problems
- A basic application of the above

I intend to expand upon what I have done here, by studying non-analytic systems. Until then, I may publish my study of Monte Carlo methods.
