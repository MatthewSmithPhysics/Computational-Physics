What is This Page?

This page documents my journey to self-learn computational physics. It is to serve as evidence to any employer or institution that I have experience in computing in the context of physics and maths. Although knowledge in this field is no doubt helpful in my studies, my programme does not directly support and assess this, so I feel that this page's existence is necessary.
